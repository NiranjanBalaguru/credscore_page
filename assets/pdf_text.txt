Title: Unified Metric for Hallucination Risk Evaluation in Vision–
Language Models 
 
 
 
1. System Overview: 
The system is designed to evaluate hallucination behaviour in Vison-Language Models (VLMs) by 
computing a unified credibility metric across clean, out-of-distribution(OOD), and adversarial 
conditions. The pipeline operates sequentially: 
 Dataset → Perturbation → Model → Semantic Scoring → Metric → Credibility →Risk 
The architecture follows a Monolithic analytical design with internally modular components, 
enabling: 
1. Controlled experimental evaluation 
2. Reproducibility 
3. Extensibility to new models and datasets 
 
2. Components: 
Component 
Function
